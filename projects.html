Welcome to PRISM

PRISM is a design fiction project shown as a short film and a simple website prototype. It explores how emotion-driven mixed reality could affect communication and mental health. The system tries to solve social anxiety by clarifying emotions, while also questioning how real our freedom is in personalized digital worlds.

Bloodroot on the Undying Country

This interactive installation visualizes society as a living system that digests and recycles class structures like nutrients. Inspired by thinkers like Spencer, Bourdieu, and Bauman, it uses Arduino-driven motion to explore how modern biopolitics maintains social order and reproduces inequality.

UNDERCLASS_HERO

Underclass Hero is a CG-driven story set in a broken San Francisco ruled by tech corporations. It follows Zared Fulk, who monitors Septer Bauner, an android who begins to show real empathy, courage, and loyalty. As people give in to fear, Septer chooses to fight for the voiceless, reminding the world what it truly means to be human.
